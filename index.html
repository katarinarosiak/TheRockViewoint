The Rock Viewpoint

The peak of the loop

The peak of the loop

Immerse yourself in the Phou Hin Boun National Protected Area, an uncharted world of ancient karstic forests, breath-taking views, and endemic wildlife in Khammouane, Central Laos. A must stop to take-in the view, or partake in an exceptional adventure.

This attraction has been developed by Laos’ leading eco-tourism company, Green Discovery Laos, with the sustainable tourism in mind. It is a must stop in the Thakek Loop that also acts as a tourist information center for all visitors in Khammouane Province.
