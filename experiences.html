Our Experiences

Highlights of the Rock


What Will You Do

Discover the Limestone Forest of Phou Pha Marn and experience the Highlights of The Rock for yourself! This experience is perfect for those who want to enjoy the highlights of the Protected Area within a short time. Accessible for almost everybody, it is recommended for families and friends.

Start the tour with an epic zip line, flying across the valley of this stunning landscape, to the thrilling circuit of via ferrata, suspended bridges, ziplines and spider net. The trip ends with an incredible fly across the rock formations on the final zipline, back to the Phou Pha Marn visitor centre where you can enjoy the views from the Khop Chai Der restaurant.

SPIDER WEB WALK

A thrilling see-through, suspended rope bridge allowing you to cross the valley as if walking on clouds. The rope is solidly bound in a number of diamond shapes.

ZIP-LINE

Cables that connect two distant points over the limestone valley. You will fly along the cable suspended in a harness attached to the cable.

VIA FERRATA

Mountain route made of strong metal staples embedded in the rock, alongside a safety cable, allowing you walk or climb the rocks with ease.

SUSPENDED BRIDGES

Canopy bridges that hang over the limestone, connecting two distant points that are inaccessible any other way.

What To Expect:

It is not a very physically challenging activity, and can easily be accomplished with a reasonable level of fitness. However, it takes place at high heights. The weight limit for all our zipline tours is 120kg. You need to be at least 120 cm tall to participate. The weather may be hot, especially in dry season, and there is very little shade. The activity can only be done wearing closed shoes.

Activities:

Zip-line, Via Ferrata, Spidernet bridge

What to bring:

Sunscreen, insect repellent comfortable clothes, closed toed shoes (mandatory!) , refillable water bottle

The Rock at Sunset


What Will You Do:

Experience Highlights of The Rock at sunset. Delve into the National Protected area when the sun is going down, dying the limestone forest in oranges and pinks. It is indeed the most special moment to experience the adventure, and one of the most beautiful sunsets to witness.
